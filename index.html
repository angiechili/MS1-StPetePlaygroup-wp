Welcome

Croeso

We provide childcare to children age 2 to 5 years in a playgroup style setting. We encourage and guide children to be themselves and have fun,providing them with a safe and familiar space where they are free to play and get involved in a range of activities, carefully curated to stimulate all areas of development.


All our staff is fully qualified in early years and trained on first aid and food hygiene and safeguarding. Why choose us?


FUN

Toys, games, stories and songs! Our weekly themes include all these plus lots of other activities that help stimulate our kids development and curiosity. Our free play philosophy means that children can join in our themed activities or take part in whatever activity they choose.

HEALTHY

From a young age! All our snacks and food are planned in line with the Healthy and Sustainable Pre School Scheme guidelines. We support independence and encourage social skills by involving children in all aspects of their snack time. From singing the snack song to doing tasks, such as cutting fruit, buttering bread and pouring drinks. This process helps them learn about healthy and fresh food and enjoy it even more.

OUTDOOR

Always an option! Children are free to play outside (supervised) at any point during the session. Both our patio and lawn garden are fitted to implement outdoor learning, through games, activities and outdoor toys.
